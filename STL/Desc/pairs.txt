Pair:
    > pair contains 2 values

    > Syntax: pair<1,2> var;
      Here 1 and 2 are data types or continers and var is variable name
      Ex: pair<int,string> p;

    > Initialization of pair:
      p = make_pair(10,"abc");
      p = {10,"abcs"};

    > Output
      p.first, p.second

    > pairs of array
      pair<int,int> p[10];
      p[0]={1,2};
      p[1]={9,6};
      p[2]={2,4};
      swap(p[0], p[2]);

    > Taking input
      cin >> p.first >> p.second;
